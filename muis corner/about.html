A bit about myself- I am a self-taught nail tech who’s nail journey began in 2020. I used to get my nails done monthly, but similarly to others, when the pandemic struck, I resorted to providing the service myself! Doing my own nails quickly became a form a therapy that I resort to when life gets overwhelming. It’s allocated time where I get to put on my favourite playlist, sit down for a few hours and focus on creating art. The name of my business- mui’s corner, represents me as a brand. In my family, I am the youngest, and mui translates to younger sister in Cantonese. mui’s corner is a place where I am able to express myself freely through nail design, and I welcome you to relax, pull up a chair, and join me in my little corner :)

about builder gel

Builder gel is best known for maintaining and rejuvenating the health, strength, and length of the natural nail. A flexable rubber base is overlayed on the nail, allowing for natural growth while protect them from breakage.

my specialty

Over the years I have explored many medias and techniques such as polygel, acrylics, shellac, builder gel, and gel-X. After discovering gel-X and builder gel, I fell in love with the methods and now specialize with the system. I love recreating trending nail sets, and always manage to incorperate a twist on the design to make it my own.

about gel-x

Gel-X extensions is a method where a full plastic tip is glued onto the natural nail. The method I practice involves builder gel, allowing for amazing retension grow out. This system is a great option for those who want added length to the natural nail.
